Classifying Dog Breed with a CNN

This project accepts any user-supplied image as input. If a dog is detected in the image, it will provide an estimate of the dog's breed. If a human is detected, it will provide an estimate of the dog breed that is most resembling. In this real-world setting, I pieced together a series of models to perform different tasks; for instance, the algorithm that detects humans in an image will be different from the CNN that infers dog breed.

  • Jupyter Notebook, VSCode
  • Python, Pandas, Numpy
  • Matplotlib
  • Keras, Scikit-learn
  • OpenCV

Predicting Bike Sharing Data

This is a forecast type of project, where I dealt with historical data from a real-world company called Cyclo Hop, whose business model consists of renting bicycles scattered around the city, they had the business problem of not finding the best total number of bikes to keep in their inventory throughout the year. For this project I created a neural network trained on historical data to make predictions about the total number of bikes to have in store over time.

Predicting Boston Housing Prices

In this project I completed all the steps from a Machine Learning project pipeline. The business problem consisted of assuming the role of a real estate agent who needs to have a good estimate of the price of houses in a particular region at the city of Boston, for this I created a Neural Network that uses the features of each house to make a prediction of its price. Bringing automation into a real-state agent's work, will increase its efficiency and reduce costs with possible miscalculations or bias.

As ferramentas utilizadas foram:

  • Python
  • Numpy and Pandas
  • Matplotlib
